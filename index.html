Hello

In the spring, the bear living in the forest woke up. When he went outside, he saw lots of flowers in bloom. After enjoying the flowers for a while, the bear celebrated the arrival of spring by playing with the branches of trees, making them into the shape of a bear.

In the summer, the bear swam in the pond and met many fish. He had fun swimming with them, playing with them, and even giving them food. He also enjoyed taking naps in the shade to escape the heat.

In the fall, the leaves of the trees in the forest started changing color. The bear walked around the forest, admiring the beautiful autumn leaves. He enjoyed the sound of crunching leaves under his feet and collected acorns and other fruits from the trees.

In the winter, the forest turned into a snowy wonderland. The bear made snowmen, had snowball fights, and ran around in the snow. He also slept for a long time in a hole in a tree to hibernate.

After a year had passed, the bear woke up in the spring and found that the forest had changed again. He reflected on the past year, enjoying the change of seasons and spending time in nature. He looked forward to spending another year in harmony with nature.
